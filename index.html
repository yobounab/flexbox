Born and raised in Algeria, I moved to New York in March 2018, where I also developed my passion for photography and, most recently, for film photography.

New York City is the perfect city to photograph. Its diverse architecture and people make it all the more special.

As I was still getting to know the city, it was fun to take a picture of things and people that I encountered everywhere I went. You can follow me on Instagram.
